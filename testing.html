What most people don't know about, though, is that it's made to encourage people to sin and rebel against God. In particular, the "Debater" is often drawn with strong devil motifs and with its attractiveness played up. This is most likely a ploy to make the devil look desirable and sexy, tempting people to sin. Not only that, the website also teaches people that they can defy God's will and passes it off as a good thing.
